How to Search

Use the search box to find parkrunners by:

  • Name
  • Age Category
  • Club
  • Gender
  • Achievement (PB or First Timer)

Start typing in the box and after two characters the matching options will appear in a drop down list. Select the option to find.

To refine the search, start typing again and matching options for the remaining categories will appear in a drop down list for selection.

To remove the search, click or tap on the X next to the selected option.

What is this table?

This table shows the complete results for the specified week at this course. It is automatically shown when you navigate to the results page, but can also be reached by clicking on Latest Results, or by picking a specific week from the Event History table. If you bookmark the Latest Results page in your browser, the bookmark will always take you to the most up to date results table. If you bookmark a results page reached via the Event History table, the bookmark will then always take you to that specific week's results.

The data

  • Position - the participant's overall position in the parkrun.
  • parkrunner - is the registered name of the parkrunner; clicking on the name takes you to that person's results page for this course.
  • Time - is the participant's recorded time - we record the overall time between the Run Director's start instruction and the finisher crossing the line.
  • Age Group - represents the age category of the participant on the day of the run; the first letter represents the major age categories (J)unior, (S)enior and (Veteran); the second letter represents gender (M)en and (W)omen; the remainder of the category represents a more specific age range. There are two further categories for wheelchair users; WWC and MWC represent Women and Men respectively.
  • Age Grade - Gives an indication of how well the parkrunner has done relative to their age and gender see the FAQ article for more information.
  • Gender - Male or Female
  • Club - Running club if registered.
  • PB - Personal Best
  • parkruns - Total number of results across parkruns of this type (5k or junior), and an indicator of membership of the various parkrun clubs.

Results can be sorted and searched using the filters at the top of the table.
