What is this table?

This table shows a summary of each of this course's parkruns.

The data

  • Event # - the run's course number; clicking on this, or the date, takes you to that particular week's results.
  • Date - the date of the parkrun.
  • Number of Finishers - Number of parkrunners who completed the course
  • Number of Volunteers - Number of volunteers who are recorded as having helped at the course.
  • First Finisher - Male and female first finishers with their times.

Results can be sorted and searched using the filters at the top of the table.
